Kayla Bell's Website

Hello, my name is Kayla Bell! Welcome to my website. 

I'm a senior at the University of Wisconsin-Madison majoring in Journalism and Political Science with a certificate in Digital Studies and a graduation date of May 2023. Over the years I've developed a love for storytelling and community, which have led me to my interest in documentary filmmaking. As you navigate through my site, you will gain a more in-depth look into who I am both within the world of journalism and outside it as well. This includes a look into my work as an amateur photographer, a variety of the publications I've written for and the articles that go along with them, and, outside of the realm of my journalistic adventures, the hobbies and interests I often partake in.
